New paper from Omri published in *iScience*!
> {{ page.citation }}

Available [here](https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S2589004225019625).

- Data from hundreds of participants elucidates how musical scales shape perception
- The geometry underlying scales influences perceptual sensitivity to note deviations
- Geometric regularity predicts perceptual sensitivity across diverse structures
- Effects persist in the absence of a familiar tuning system
