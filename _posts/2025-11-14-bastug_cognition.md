New paper from Berfin published in *Cognition*!
> {{ page.citation }}

Available [here](https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S0010027725002914).

- Individual exemplars of natural auditory textures can be memorized when repeated.
- Temporally local features can co-exist with time-averaged summary statistics in the representations of natural textures.
- Multi-scale representations of complex sounds could be an efficient way to adapt to different task demands.
